# Sport Psychology Flash Cards

A dark-themed interactive flashcard website for studying sport psychology test material.

## Features

- **Dark Theme**: Eye-friendly dark color scheme perfect for extended study sessions
- **Four Chapters**: Complete coverage of all study guide topics
  - Chapter 14: Imagery
  - Chapter 16: Goal Setting
  - Chapter 10: Leadership
  - Chapter 9: Team Dynamics & Cohesion
- **Interactive Cards**: Click any card to flip and reveal the answer
- **Chapter Navigation**: Easy switching between different chapters
- **Progress Tracking**: See total cards and current chapter

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Click on a chapter button at the top to switch between topics
3. Click on any flashcard to flip it and see the answer
4. Click again to flip back to the question
5. Study through all cards in each chapter

## Content Coverage

### Chapter 14: Imagery
- Definition of mental imagery
- Internal vs. external imagery
- Why athletes use imagery
- Purposes and uses of imagery
- Explanations of how imagery works
- Keys to imagery (vividness and controllability)

### Chapter 16: Goal Setting
- Goal setting principles
- SMARTS goals framework
- Common problems and solutions
- Outcome, performance, and process goals
- Why goal setting sometimes fails

### Chapter 10: Leadership
- Definition and differences from management
- Trait, behavioral, and interactional approaches
- Leadership effectiveness and behaviors
- Four components of effective leadership (Weinberg & Gould)
- Relationship vs. task-oriented leaders
- Transformational leadership components
- John Wooden's principles

### Chapter 9: Team Dynamics & Cohesion
- Social loafing and conditions
- Team development stages (forming, storming, norming, performing)
- Teambuilding suggestions
- Steiner's model of team dynamics
- Task vs. social cohesion
- Maximizing individual performance in teams
